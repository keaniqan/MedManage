Things they "can" do:

Superadmin -  1. Insert/Edit/View/Delete admin, doctor and patient users
              3. View Audit log
              4. View medical history

Admin -       1. Insert/Delete/Search doctor and patient users
              2. View/Edit the user table

Doctor -      1. Insert/Search/Delete patient users
              2. Insert/Search/Edit/Delete patient's medical records:
                a. Add/Edit/Delete medicines to patient's schedules
                b. Add/View/Search/Edit/Delete prescription to patient's schedules
                c. View/Search available medicine
                d. Insert/View/Search/Edit/Delete schedules for patients
                e. Insert/View/Search/Edit/Delete reminders for patients according to their schedules
              3. Insert/View/Search/Edit/Delete appointments

Patient -     1. View their own medication schedules/reminder
              2. View their own medical records
              3. Add side effect
              4. View/Edit their own profile
              5. View appointment schedule
              6. Request for an appointment if a side effect has been identified

Things they "cannot" do

Superadmin -  

Admin -       1. Cannot View/Edit/Delete/Search the patient's medical history
              2. Cannot create other admin users

Doctor -      1. Cannot Insert/Search/Delete other doctor users
              2. Cannot edit other user profiles except their own doctor profile


Patient -     1. Cannot Insert/Search/Delete other doctor users
