Things they "can" do:

Superadmin -  1. Insert/Update/View/Delete admin, doctor and patient users
              3. View Audit log
              4. View medical history

Admin -       1. Insert/Delete/View doctor and patient users
              2. View/Update the user table

Doctor -      1. Insert/View/Delete their assigned patient users
              2. Insert/View/Update/Delete their assigned patient's details:
                a. Add/Update/Delete medicines to patient's prescriptions
                b. View available medicine
                c. Insert/View/Update/Delete reminders for patients according to their prescriptions (can even edit patient's reminders)
                e. Add symptoms/disease patient is suffering from
              3. Insert/View/Update/Delete appointments

Patient -     1. View their own medication schedules
              2. View their own medical records
              3. Add side effect
              4. View/Update their own profile
              5. View appointment schedule
              6. Request for an appointment if a side effect has been identified
              7. Can also create/edit their own reminders (not created by doctor)
              8. Can update when they take the medicine (for compliance)

Things they "cannot" do

Admin -       1. Cannot View/Update/Delete the patient's details
              2. Cannot Insert/Update/Delete other admin users

Doctor -      1. Cannot Insert/View/Delete other doctor users
              2. Cannot Update other user profiles except their own doctor profile
              3. Cannot View/Edit/Delete other patient's medical records who are not assigned to them

Patient -     1. Cannot Insert/View/Delete any other users except themselves
              2. Cannot Insert/Update/Delete prescriptions, medication, appointment
